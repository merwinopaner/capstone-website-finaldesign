Possible Careers in Multimedia

Explore the wide range of creative paths you can pursue in the world of multimedia arts. Discover where your creativity can take you!

Digital Artist

A creative professional who specializes in the use of digital technology. Their canvas is the computer screen, and their brush can range from styluses to software that mimics traditional art techniques in a virtual environment

Possible careers for Digital Artist:

Cartoon Artist, Book Illustrator, Comic Book Artist, Book Cover Artist, Graphic Designer, Logo Designer, Motion Graphic Designer, Storyboard Artist, Video Game Designer, and more!

Graphic Designer

Uses visual elements like images, colors, typography, and layout to communicate messages and ideas. They design a wide range of materials, including logos, advertisements, brochures, websites, and more. Their goal is to make content visually engaging while also clear and effective for the audience.

Possible careers for Graphic Designer:

Graphic Designer, Advertising Art Director, Creative Director, UX Designer, Packaging Designer, Logo Designer, and more!

Motion Designer

A creative who specializes in creating animated graphics by using design, animation, and visual effects techniques to bring static images or illustrations to life on a screen.

Possible careers for Motion Designer:

Motion Designer, Motion Graphic Artist, Graphic Designer, Animator, Art Director, Visual Effects Artist, Video Editor, and more!

2D Animator

Create designs to tell a story. They use their artistic skills to produce images, such as logos or graphics, that simulate movement in a two dimensional space.

Possible careers for 2D Animator:

2D Animator, Lead Animator, Character Animator, Concept Artist, Storyboard Artist, and more!

3D Animator

A specialized professional within the digital arts and entertainment industries who focuses on creating three-dimensional moving images and animations.

Possible careers for 3D Animator:

3D Animator, Storyboard Artist, Game Designer, Environment Artist, Background Artist, and more!

Film Director

Manages the creative aspects of the production. They direct the making of a film by visualizing the script while guiding the actors and technical crew to capture the vision for the screen. 

Possible careers for Film Director:

Film Director, Assistant Director, Screenwriter, Cinematographer, Film Producer, Film, Editor, and more!

Sound Engineer

Audio professionals whose work is vital to the creation of movies, video games, concerts and albums. These skilled individuals choose or create music, dialogue and sound effects to deliver an impactful final product.

Possible careers for Sound Engineer:

Sound Engineer, Audio Technician, Music Producer, Acoustical Engineer, Foley Artist, Broadcast Engineer, and more!

Post-Prod Professional

Enhance audio, visual, and motion graphics after filming has been completed. Editing software can be used to add titles or create transitions between shots.

Possible careers for Post-Prod Professional:

Video Editor, Post Production Supervisor, Videographer, Video Director, Production Assistant, Motion Graphic Designer, and more!

UI/UX Designer

UI design focuses on creating the visual interface of apps, websites, and digital products, shaping users’ first impressions. UI designers specialize in digital interfaces, while UX designers handle the overall user experience from design and usability to functionality.

Possible careers for UI/UX Designer:

UI/UX Designer, UX Researcher, Interaction Designer, UX Consulting, UX Specialist, and more!

Game Developer

Game developer helps transform games from a concept to a playable reality. They do this by coding visual elements and features, and testing iterations until a game is ready for market.

Possible careers for Game Developer:

Game Developer, Game Programmer, Game Play Engineer, Game Play Animator, Game Designer, and more!

Game Designer

A game designer plays a central role in the video game development process. These individuals are responsible for shaping the overall concept, gameplay mechanics, and user experience of a video game.

Possible careers for Game Designer:

Game Designer, Mobile App Designer, Special Effects Artist, Game Tester, Character Designer, Game Developer and more!
